ABOUT THE SOCIETY

Our Manifesto

Founded in the underground spaces where art meets revolution, Society of the Cellar is a collective of theatre makers dedicated to dismantling conventions. We create work that exists in the liminal space between performance and provocation.

Our productions excavate forgotten histories, challenge dominant narratives, and reimagine theatrical form. We believe in theatre as a radical act—a space where audiences don't just watch, but participate in collective transformation.

"Theatre should disturb the comfortable and comfort the disturbed."

Since 2025

Radical Aesthetic

We reject traditional staging in favor of immersive, site-specific experiences that blur boundaries between performer and audience.

Historical Subversion

Our work re-examines history through marginalized perspectives, revealing suppressed narratives and challenging official accounts.

Collective Practice

We operate as a non-hierarchical collective where all members contribute to the creative process and decision-making.

OUR COLLABORATORS

A rotating collective of theatre makers, visual artists, and radical thinkers who bring our visions to life.

JAN-DAVID SOUTAR

Founding Director

Creator of our debut production Oh Constantine!. Explore more at jandavidsoutar.com.

DARIUS CHEN

Sound Designer

Darius creates immersive soundscapes that blend field recordings with electronic composition. His work has been featured at the Berliner Festspiele.

ELENA PETROVA

Movement Director

Elena's choreography explores the politics of the body under surveillance. Former member of Pina Bausch's Tanztheater Wuppertal.

MARCUS ABEL

Dramaturg

Marcus specializes in historical research and radical reinterpretations of classical texts. Author of "Theatre of the Oppressed: A New Grammar."

SOFIA MARTINEZ

Lighting Designer

Sofia creates lighting environments that transform architectural spaces. Her work has been described as "painting with darkness."
